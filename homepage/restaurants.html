Restaurants

Harlem is home to a variety of foods from a variety of different cultures. But by far the best type of food to get when in the neighborhood is their Soul Food, an ethnic cuisine traditionally prepared and eaten by African Americans, originating in the Southern United States. Some classic dishes include:

  • Fried chicken, fish, and pork
  • Cornbread
  • Black-eyed peas
  • Candied yams
  • Macaroni and cheese
  • Collard greens
  • Sweet potato pie
